What is "Storage Cost" vs "Ordering Cost"?

Storage Cost: It costs money to keep items sitting in a warehouse.
Ordering Cost: It costs money to make delivery trucks run back and forth.

Our AI automatically finds the perfect mathematical balance so you don't order too rarely (high storage cost) or too often (high truck cost).
